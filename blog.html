Fighting Hunger Together

In a world with as much luxury as ours, nobody should have to go hungry. However, the unfortunate truth is that there are currently over 821 million people who are hungry. That means that 1 in 9 individuals on the planet do not have enough to eat. A nation's nutritional resilience is directly impacted by its economic resilience, much like the poverty-hunger cycle. We are transforming the way that poverty and hunger are perceived here at The Hunger Project.
