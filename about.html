We promote a national network of food banks that collectively offer emergency food and help to those confined to Poverty. We also gather excess food from all kinds of food suppliers and deliver staright to charities, giving much-needed support to vulnerable individuals and charities.
Our mission is to ensure that no one in the neighbourhood goes to bed hungry. The primary focus of “Serving Smiles” will be to provide immediate relief by offering two distinct donation options: one-time contributions and recurring donations. Our goal will be to enable individuals to contribute in a meaningful way, ensuring that those facing hunger receive assistance, particularly before bedtime.
Our goal is to create a smooth online platform that will encourage community involvement by enabling everyone to contribute to the help of individuals who are unable to care for themselves due to a variety of obstacles. We hope to create a network of kind people who are dedicated to improving the lives of others through our website.
